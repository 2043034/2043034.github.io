About

Welcome to my (2043034) personal website! I'm 2043034, a web designer, computer programmer, penetration tester, and Linux enthusiast. I am part of the shadowbox organization, and was one of the main developers for the site and for the organization. However, because of certain issues, the shadowbox server is down, indefinitely. I, along with a couple other members, tried to bring it back up, but so far we have been unable to find a server or host right for our needs. In the absence of the shadowbox website, I decided it would be a good idea to start hosting my own site, a sort of clone of the shadowbox site. It will contain some of the shadowbox content, but also a lot of my own stuff. I do not have the capabilities to host my own server, as the founder of shadowbox, Sdev, did. So, I turned to the next best thing: GitHub. It offers blazing fast speeds, and is really easy to set up and maintain: no server setup needed. This site is really just a place to display my content and share my knowledge, so just take a look around, and hopefully you'll find something interesting!
